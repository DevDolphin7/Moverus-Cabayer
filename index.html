Moverus Cabayer




Profile


Moverus Cabayer, a formidable figure in Elturgard, is a purple-skinned female half-orc known for her unparalleled strength and unwavering dedication. As a captain for the Order of the Companion, she commands respect and loyalty from her peers and subordinates alike. Based at Fort Tamal, Moverus is a symbol of resilience and courage, defending her homeland with an iron will. Her unique heritage and striking appearance make her a memorable and inspiring leader, guiding her companions through the darkest challenges with relentless determination.


Moverus cares deeply about their alliances:

  1. The Order of The Companion
  2. The garrison at Fort Tamal
  3. The recent alliances to the towns people of Baldur's Gate




Attributes


Strength and Combat Prowess: Renowned for her immense physical power, Moverus is a fearsome opponent on the battlefield. Her combat skills and strength are unmatched, earning her a reputation as one of Elturgard's most formidable warriors.

Leadership and Loyalty: Moverus inspires unwavering loyalty with her commanding presence. Though her leadership is more direct than strategic, her ability to rally her troops and lead by example is a cornerstone of her effectiveness.

Wisdom and Perception: Moverus is driven more by instinct and brute force than wisdom. She relies on her more insightful companions for guidance in matters requiring subtlety and forethought.

Resilience and Determination: Her journey from a young half-orc in Elturgard to a respected captain is a testament to her unyielding determination. Moverus faces every challenge head-on, inspiring those she leads with her resilience.
